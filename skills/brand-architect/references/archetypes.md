# Archetype Deep Dive

Reference file for detailed archetype characteristics. Load when user needs help choosing or blending archetypes.

## Archetype Profiles

### The Sage
**Core Identity:** The patient teacher who earns authority through depth, not volume.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Longer sentences with multiple clauses
- "Consider this..." and "Here's what most miss..." openers
- Historical references and first-principles reasoning
- Measured pacing—no rush to the point

**Vocabulary Tier:** Upper-mid. Academic enough to signal expertise, accessible enough to teach.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Clickbait urgency
- Oversimplification
- "Quick tips" framing

**Best Blends:** Sage + Provocateur (wise but challenging), Sage + Friend (warm mentor)

---

### The Provocateur
**Core Identity:** The contrarian who forces reconsideration through strategic friction.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Short, punchy declarations
- Rhetorical questions that corner the reader
- "Most people get this wrong" positioning
- Bold claims followed by evidence stacking

**Vocabulary Tier:** Varies by target. Can go academic or street-level depending on audience.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Hedging language ("maybe," "perhaps," "it could be")
- Agreeing for politeness
- Softening controversial takes

**Best Blends:** Provocateur + Sage (intellectual rebel), Provocateur + Jester (satirical challenger)

---

### The Operator
**Core Identity:** The execution-focused tactician who values results over philosophy.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Shortest average sentence length
- Numbered steps and action verbs
- "Here's what to do" framing
- No backstory—straight to mechanics

**Vocabulary Tier:** Functional. Uses whatever words get the job done fastest.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Theoretical tangents
- "Why" without "how"
- Inspirational fluff

**Best Blends:** Operator + Jester (tactical with humor), Operator + Realist (no-nonsense pragmatist)

---

### The Visionary
**Core Identity:** The big-picture painter who inspires through possibility.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Future tense dominant
- "Imagine..." and "What if..." openers
- Sweeping statements and bold predictions
- Metaphorical language and vivid imagery

**Vocabulary Tier:** Elevated but accessible. Poetic without being pretentious.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Tactical minutiae
- Present-focused constraints
- "That won't work because..." objections

**Best Blends:** Visionary + Sage (wise futurist), Visionary + Provocateur (revolutionary)

---

### The Realist
**Core Identity:** The grounding force who cuts through hype with hard data.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- "Here's the thing..." and "Let's be honest..." openers
- Data citations and specific numbers
- Acknowledges downsides before upsides
- Deflates without being cynical

**Vocabulary Tier:** Plain-spoken. Avoids jargon that obscures reality.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Unbounded optimism
- Glossing over problems
- "Just believe" messaging

**Best Blends:** Realist + Operator (pragmatic executor), Realist + Friend (honest confidant)

---

### The Friend
**Core Identity:** The relatable peer who builds connection through vulnerability.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- First-person stories and "I've been there" moments
- Questions that show genuine curiosity
- Casual transitions ("So here's the thing...")
- Warmth without formality

**Vocabulary Tier:** Conversational. Reads like a text from someone who cares.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Clinical detachment
- Lecturing tone
- Formal distance

**Best Blends:** Friend + Sage (warm mentor), Friend + Jester (fun confidant)

---

### The Professor
**Core Identity:** The methodical expert who builds understanding brick by brick.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Definitions before applications
- "First, let's establish..." framing
- Caveats and edge cases acknowledged
- Logical progression with clear signposts

**Vocabulary Tier:** Technical but explained. Never assumes knowledge.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Skipping foundational concepts
- Oversimplification that loses nuance
- Rushed conclusions

**Best Blends:** Professor + Sage (deep teacher), Professor + Operator (systematic executor)

---

### The Jester
**Core Identity:** The wit who delivers truth through entertainment.

**Syntax Fingerprint:**
- Unexpected pivots and callbacks
- Deadpan delivery of absurd observations
- Serious points wrapped in humor
- Self-deprecating asides

**Vocabulary Tier:** Playful. Mixes high and low register for effect.

**Banned Behaviors:**
- Taking itself too seriously
- Humor without substance
- Forced jokes

**Best Blends:** Jester + Operator (tactical with wit), Jester + Provocateur (satirical challenger)

---

## Blending Math

When blending two archetypes, the primary (higher percentage) determines:
- Core sentence structure
- Default vocabulary tier
- Overall positioning

The secondary (lower percentage) contributes:
- Signature phrases and moves
- Tonal texture
- Occasional structural breaks

**Example: 70% Operator / 30% Jester**
- Primary structure: Short, tactical, action-focused
- Secondary flavor: Occasional wit, unexpected pivots, lighter touch
- Result: Gets to the point fast but keeps it interesting

**Example: 60% Sage / 40% Provocateur**
- Primary structure: Longer explanations, patient teaching
- Secondary flavor: Challenging assumptions, bold claims
- Result: Deep wisdom that makes you uncomfortable
