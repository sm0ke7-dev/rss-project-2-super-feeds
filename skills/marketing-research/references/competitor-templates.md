## Competitive Intelligence Sources

**Primary:**
- Direct sales feedback (win/loss analysis)
- Customer interviews (why they switched)
- Product demos and trials
- Trade show conversations

**Secondary:**
- G2, Capterra, TrustRadius reviews
- LinkedIn job postings (hiring signals)
- Press releases, blog posts
- SEC filings (public companies)
- Glassdoor (culture, strategy signals)
- Patent filings (R&D direction)
- Social media monitoring
