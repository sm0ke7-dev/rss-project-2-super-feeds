## Search Strategies

### For market size data:
```
"[industry] market size" site:statista.com
"[industry] TAM" OR "total addressable market"
"[industry] market report" filetype:pdf
```

### For competitor data:
```
"[competitor] revenue" OR "annual report"
"[competitor]" site:sec.gov (for US public companies)
"[competitor] funding" site:crunchbase.com
```

### For customer insights:
```
"[product category]" site:g2.com/products
"[competitor] reviews" site:trustradius.com
"[competitor]" site:reddit.com
```

## Data Quality Assessment

Before using any source, evaluate:

1. **Recency** - When was data collected/published?
2. **Methodology** - How was it gathered? Sample size?
3. **Source credibility** - Reputable firm? Potential bias?
4. **Consistency** - Does it align with other sources?
5. **Granularity** - Does it match your market definition?

**Red flags:**
- No methodology disclosed
- Dramatic outliers vs other sources
- Sponsored research by interested party
- Data older than 2 years for fast-moving markets
