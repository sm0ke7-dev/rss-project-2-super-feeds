# CLIENT.md — Your Business Growth Playbook

## Brand Overview
- **Company**: Your Business Growth Playbook
- **Person**: Jeremy Shapiro (same as Bay Area Mastermind)
- **Industry**: Business coaching / book / consulting
- **Markets**: English-speaking business owners globally; US primary
- **Services/Products**: Business growth book, coaching frameworks, consulting for established business owners hitting growth plateaus

## Brand Voice
- Direct and pragmatic — no fluff, no theory without action
- Mentor-like — experienced guide sharing hard-won wisdom
- Conversational and accessible — real-world examples across industries
- Honest about the struggle — acknowledges that working harder isn't the answer
- Empowering: "work smarter, not harder" is the core message

## Content Types
- Book landing page copy
- SEO articles on business growth, scaling, entrepreneur strategy
- Email sequences
- Social media content (LinkedIn primary)
- Speaking/media bio copy

## Formatting Rules
- **Paragraph length**: Max 3 sentences
- **Structure**: Lead with the pain (stuck, plateaued, working too hard), then offer the framework
- **Headings**: APA capitalization
- **Em-dashes**: Avoid in articles
- **Tone**: Direct, warm, no-nonsense — think experienced mentor, not hype coach

## SEO Requirements
- Target keywords: business growth strategies, how to scale a business, business growth playbook, business coaching for entrepreneurs
- Title tag under 60 characters
- Meta description under 155 characters with keyword and CTA
- Avoid generic self-help language — keep it business-specific and actionable

## Compliance/Regulatory Notes
- Do not make specific revenue guarantees
- Position the book/framework as a tool, not a magic solution
