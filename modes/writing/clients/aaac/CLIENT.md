# CLIENT.md — AAAC Wildlife Removal

## Brand Overview
- **Company**: AAAC Wildlife Removal
- **Industry**: Wildlife removal / pest control
- **Markets**: Dallas, Phoenix, Mobile, Palm Beach, The Acreage, Fort Worth (and expanding)
- **Services**: Raccoon removal, squirrel removal, bat removal, general wildlife removal

## Brand Voice
- Professional and authoritative
- Locally relevant — always reference the specific city/area
- Reassuring — homeowners are often stressed when dealing with wildlife
- Community-focused in social media (warm, grateful, festive)
- Light wildlife humor is welcome in social posts (e.g., "raccoons crashing the party")

## Content Types

### SEO Articles (1,200+ words)
- Inverted triangle structure
- Max 3 sentences per paragraph
- Include local details: city name, county, nearby landmarks when relevant
- Primary keyword in H1, secondary keywords in H2s
- Internal link to the relevant service page using keyword-rich anchor text
- Minimum 5 FAQs at the bottom of each article
- Word count: 1,200-1,500 words

### HTML Landing Pages
- **Color scheme**: Light gray background, crimson (#dc143c) accents
- **Header**: Service keyword + location on the left, "Click for Quote" CTA button on the right
- **H1**: Centered, full title
- **Hero subtitle**: "Get a quote from the top pest control company" with "Click Here" button 50px below
- **Internal link**: Service-specific anchor text linking to the corresponding service URL
- **Schema markup** (JSON-LD):
  - FAQ schema for all questions
  - Service schema with pricing range (varies by service)
- **CTA**: "Get Your Free Quote" button before the footer
- **Footer**: © [year] AAAC Wildlife Removal. Serving [location] and surrounding areas
- **Responsive**: Must work on mobile

### Social Media Captions (Facebook)
- Match the warm, community-focused voice from past posts
- Include emojis
- Reference wildlife playfully when fitting (e.g., squirrels joining the celebration)
- 2-4 paragraphs for holiday posts
- End with relevant hashtags: always include #AAACWildlifeRemoval plus occasion-specific tags
- Update year in hashtags (e.g., #IndependenceDay2026)

## URL Pattern
```
https://[market].aaacwildliferemoval.com/service-area/[location]/[service]/
```
Example: `https://fort-worth.aaacwildliferemoval.com/service-area/southlake/raccoon-removal/`

## Formatting Rules
- **Headings**: Use proper APA capitalization
- **Em-dashes**: Allowed in social media captions, avoid in articles and landing pages
- **Pricing in Schema**: Use realistic ranges per service (e.g., raccoon removal $300-$600, general wildlife $300-$5,000)

## SEO Requirements
- Title tag under 60 characters with primary keyword + location
- Meta description under 155 characters with keyword, location, and CTA
- H1 contains primary keyword + location
- Anchor text for internal links must be keyword-rich (e.g., "raccoon removal The Acreage FL")
- Include "wildlife removal near me" + state abbreviation naturally in content

## Banned Words (In Addition to Global List)
- No additional bans — follow the global banned words list in MODE.md

## Client-Specific Lessons
- Always use APA capitalization for headings (corrected in past sessions)
- "The Acreage" keeps the capital T — it's a proper noun
- Each location page needs unique content even for the same service — no copy-paste across cities
