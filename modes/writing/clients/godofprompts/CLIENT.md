# CLIENT.md — God of Prompts

## Brand Overview
- **Company**: God of Prompt (godofprompt.ai)
- **Industry**: AI tools / digital products
- **Markets**: Global — primarily English-speaking, digitally savvy audiences
- **Services/Products**: AI prompt libraries (30,000+ prompts), Mega-Bundle packs, Custom GPTs, n8n automation bundles, prompt guides for ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Gemini, Grok

## Brand Voice
- Bold and confident — "God of Prompt" is a strong brand name, lean into it
- Energetic and hype-forward without being spammy
- Practical and results-oriented — the product saves time and boosts productivity
- Accessible to both beginners and advanced AI users
- Light authority tone — positioned as THE source for AI prompts

## Content Types
- Product landing pages
- Email copy
- Social media content
- Blog/SEO articles on AI productivity and prompt engineering
- Ad copy

## Formatting Rules
- **Paragraph length**: Max 2-3 sentences — punchy and scannable
- **Structure**: Hook-first, benefits-driven
- **Headings**: APA capitalization
- **Em-dashes**: Allowed
- **Bullets**: Use liberally for features and benefits
- **Numbers**: Lead with numbers where possible (e.g., "2,000+ prompts", "Save 10 hours a week")

## SEO Requirements
- Target informational and commercial intent keywords around AI prompts, ChatGPT productivity, prompt engineering
- Title tag under 60 characters
- Meta description under 155 characters with keyword and CTA
- Content should naturally mention specific AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, etc.)

## Compliance/Regulatory Notes
- Digital product — all sales generally final
- Do not make income claims or guarantee specific productivity outcomes
- Avoid implying the product replaces human judgment entirely

- No additions — follow global list
